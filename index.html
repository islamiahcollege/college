College Programs and Community Engagement

The college offers an updated curriculum, extension activities, research, and career counseling to support student growth.

  • Curriculum

    The college updates its curriculum every three years for undergraduate programs and every two years for postgraduate programs, following the UGC's model curriculum. We embrace the philosophy of "Think Globally, Act Locally" in our educational approach.

  • Extension Activities

    Extension Activities at our college involve collaboration among students, staff, and support services. These initiatives include life-saving services like blood donation camps, cleanliness drives, health checkups, and tree planting programs on and off campus.

  • Research

    Research Activity is thriving at our college across various departments, including History, Mathematics, Physics, Biotechnology, Biochemistry Commerce, English, Tamil, and Urdu. These departments are recognized by Affiliating University for research initiatives.

  • Employment

    The Career Guidance & Counselling Centre was established to inform students about different job opportunities & support. Students aiming for higher education receive helpful advice. Special focus is placed on assisting graduates in finding fitting employment.
